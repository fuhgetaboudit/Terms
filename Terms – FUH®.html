Shipping Rates

Worldwide shipping available. All international and domestic shipment pricing is based upon weight. International shipments may be subject to duties and taxes. International customers from select countries have the option to prepay duties and taxes at checkout. If you do not choose to prepay these duties and taxes or you do not have the option at checkout, you will likely be subject to pay upon importing into your country which is out of control.

 

Order Processing

Please allow 4-7 days to process all orders. Once your order is shipped you will receive a tracking number via email. Please allow 2-5 business days to arrive once shipped for domestic orders and an additional 10-15 days for international orders.* We are not responsible for any lost, stolen, or undelivered packages. If your package is marked as delivered but you have not received it, please open an investigation with the carrier. We will not replace any packages. If you have any questions regarding your order email info@fugazi.net.


*Due to the spread of COVID-19, some orders may be delayed for reasons beyond our control.

Return Policy

All sales are final. No refunds or exchanges. If you were sent the wrong item or if the product is defective, please contact us via email within 7 days of receiving your order.

 

Bot Policy

We reserve the right to cancel your order for any reason. If we suspect that you are using a bot or checkout link, your order may be automatically canceled. Please access our shop from the home page when new items are released.
